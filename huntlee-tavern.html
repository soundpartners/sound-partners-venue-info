Notes

Stage setup: Stage should be set up prior to act arrival. If it is not, notify bar staff to set up the stage.
Saturdays (Duos): Normal program consists of a duo from 7:30pm and a DJ during the last break and at the conclusion of the set. Duos please set up on the RIGHT 2/3 of the stage (from crowd perspective), leaving a 1.5m × 1.5m area clear on the left front. The DJ will use this space.
Sundays: Appropriate volume early in the set (mix of bistro and diners).

Access & Parking

Best parking is in the hotel car park, as close as possible to the bistro double-doors.
