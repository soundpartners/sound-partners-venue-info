Notes

Sound setup: Act to plug into the XLR auxiliary port located to the lower right hand side of the big screen. This allows the venue to extend sound internally and provides the majority of volume.
Venue has noise restrictions for beer garden performances and relies on the in-house PA for the majority of volume. Front-of-house will be low volume (or likely not needed if using monitors). Consult manager on duty if unsure.
Style note: Not a typical 'pub rock' venue — fresh and current styles are definitely appreciated!

Access & Parking
